üé≤ Basic Gameplay

  • Roll dice to move around the board clockwise
  • Buy properties you land on if they're unowned
  • Pay rent when landing on other players' properties
  • Collect $200 when passing or landing on GO

üè† Building & Development

  • Own all properties in a color group to build houses
  • Build evenly - can't have more than 1 house difference
  • Upgrade to hotels after 4 houses
  • Higher development = higher rent!

üèÜ Winning

  • Last player remaining wins
  • Players go bankrupt when they can't pay debts
